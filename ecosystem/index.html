Benefits of Unofficial Ecosystem Registration

Get involved with the THETA 360 development ecosystem

Special Access

Access to beta cloud services to help you plan and test your next business application. Past examples include RICOH Cloud API beta access. Potential for beta access to the RICOH business site for 360 media and videos. Potential for early information on firmware upgrade features. Does not include early access to the camera.

Demos and Events

Industrial demo developer walkthrough to get businesses started with the API and displaying 360 media. Listing of events that RICOH will be exhibiting at so you can stop by and discuss the latest developments.

Discounts

Unlock special discounts for partners based on your involvement with the ecosystem. Qualify for discounts based on testing theta360.biz or incorporating the THETA into events or classes.
