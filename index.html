Who runs this site?


Jesse Casman and Craig Oda went to a TechCrunch Disrupt SF Hackathon in 2015 and built an initial Unofficial API Guide to help out our friends who were launching the amazing THETA S 360 camera. The documention was popular and we uploaded it to GitHub Pages. Although we're not professional developers and we don't work for RICOH, we were able to collect and organize content from the community. We work at Oppkey, a developers relations firm in San Francisco. We do consult for RICOH from time to time, but this is not a RICOH site and is not authorized by RICOH. It's a community site by developers for developers. If there's a problem, blame us, not them. Expect problems. For the real information, please use the official RICOH documentation. Please tell us what would be useful for you.
